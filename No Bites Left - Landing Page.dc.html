Our story

It began with an apple pie.

No Bites Left started in the kitchen of Alfian, from MasterChef Indonesia Season 10. The Apple Pie was the very first signature — US apples, balanced cinnamon and a hint of nutmeg, baked until the crust shattered just right.

That one pie turned into a craving, and the craving turned into a menu. Cookies came next, then the fudgy brownies — and we're still just getting started.
